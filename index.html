About

FocusFirst helps you stay focused when you work on your computer by restricting access to distracting websites until you submit a copy of your finished assignment.

Optical Character Recognition

We use Mathpix's Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to generate a transcript of the assignment and your work whether it printed or handwritten.

Generative Pre-trained Transformer

We use OpenAI's Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT) to determine if adequate effort or progress has been put forth on your task.
